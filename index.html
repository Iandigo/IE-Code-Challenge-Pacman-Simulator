Pacman Simulator

DESCRIPTION:

The application is a simulation of Pacman moving on in a grid, of dimensions 5 units x 5 units. There are no other obstructions on the grid. Pacman is free to roam around the surface of the grid, but must be prevented from moving off the grid. Any movement that would result in Pacman moving off the grid must be prevented, however further valid movement commands must still be allowed.

COMMANDS:

PLACE X,Y,F: put the Pacman on the grid in positon X,Y and facing NORTH,SOUTH, EAST or WEST. The origin (0,0) can be considered the SOUTH WEST most corner.

MOVE: move Pacman one unit forward in the direction it is currently facing.

LEFT: rotate Pacman 90 degrees in the left direction without changing the position of Pacman.

RIGHT: rotate Pacman 90 degrees in the right direction without changing the position of Pacman.

REPORT: announce the X,Y and F of Pacman.
